\section{Master Method}
	Let T(n) be a monotonically increasing function.\\
	\\
	\\
	If $$T(n) \leq aT(n/b) + O(n^d)$$
	\\
	Then,
	\[ T(n) = 
		\begin{cases}
		O(n^d log n) & \quad \text{if } a = b^d\\
		O(n^d) & \quad \text{if } a < b^d\\
		O(n^{\log_{b}a}) & \quad \text{if } a > b^d\\
		\end{cases}
	\]
